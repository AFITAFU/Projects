CODEBITS ACADEMY

Codebits Academy is a training centre for software engineers and IT professionals located in Ota, Nigeria. It provides free and paid software development courses and supplies the software industry with highly-skilful software developers, QA engineers and IT professionals. Codebits Academy performs training in Web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Angular), .NET technologies (C#, ASP.NET Core), QA (Software Testing), databases and Web development in PHP/MySQL/Apache. Codebits Academy was created to give opportunities to students and graduates who want to be part of the next wave of technology innovation. We offer courses that take graduates from zero knowledge to having the skill set to launch their career in IT. We also provide opportunities for elite students to work alongside our senior software engineers on projects to design and develop innovative software solutions, integrations and applications. With expert tuition and excellent training facilities for students – we empower our graduates to do wonderful things.

WHO WE ARE
